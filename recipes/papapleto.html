Hot to make a papapleto


A papapleto (Source: Link El Palenke )

Ingredients

  • Hot dog bread
  • Avocados
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayonnaise
  • Fries (you can make it yourself or buy them)
  • Ketchup, Mustard or any dressing you love

Preparation

  1. Make the fries by chopping potatoes and fry them in a pan or if you bought them pre-fried you can do this in a oven, air-frier or how you like them the most.
  2. Cut the tomatoes in little cubes.
  3. Make an avocado paste (make sure that the avocados are ripe).
  4. Put the bread in a oven for less than 5 minutes, just to heat them nicely
  5. To make a papapleto just open the bread, put the fries in the bottom, add tomato and avocado paste.
  6. Over that you can add mayo, Ketchup, Mustard or any dressing you like.
  7. Enjoy!
